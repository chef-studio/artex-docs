## Swap Token on ArtexSwap V2 with Security Aspect

1. Token Swap with Aspect

<Image src="/v2/v2p5_swap.png" alt="Swap with Aspect" width={800} height={800} />

We provide 3 types of Security Aspect:
- **Black List Aspect**: Blocks dangerous accounts listed in our blacklist.
- **Anti-rug Aspect**: Prevents token supply increases that could rug pull pools.
- **High Slippage Aspect**: Prevents swaps with high slippage, estimated around 30%.

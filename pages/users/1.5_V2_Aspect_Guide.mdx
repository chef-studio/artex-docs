# ArtexSwap V2 Guide - Bind Aspects

## Add New Pool

Go to the pool page:

https://app.artexswap.xyz/pool

1. Use Add Liquidity Button.

<Image src="https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/1*wsDgnJciZn32TJdnvtQRDw.png" alt="Add Liquidity" width={800} height={800} />

2. Select your token to Add Pool. (You have to issue your token and search on input field, balabala...)

<Image src="https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/1*HqJtDcGz0W0v6VU0x3cDYQ.png" alt="Choose Token" width={800} height={800} />

3. Enter Pool. Input your initial liquidity.

<Image src="https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/1*vIvZMOwf1T1I8Uhw085flA.png" alt="Initial Liquidity" width={800} height={800} />

4. Enter My Liquidity. You will find your pool in the list.

<Image src="https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/1*s5bT2wCfP-qDBuYIkzgJ_w.png" alt="My Liquidity" width={800} height={800} />

5. In Your Pool(You will find you are pool owner), you can bind Security Aspect to your pool.

<Image src="https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/1*fJhsQSPOic86yLeNklPo4A.png" alt="Pool Owner" width={800} height={800} />

6. Sending Transaction to Bind Aspect.

<Image src="https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/1*2XPrmndkBSwviz7OTQyLHA.png" alt="Bind Aspect" width={300} height={500} />

7. You can find Aspect Binded in your Pool Page.

<Image src="https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/1*r5Z6X9SCX4-PyXxFB9SdaQ.png" alt="Binded Asepct" width={800} height={800} />
